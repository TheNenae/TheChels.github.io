TheChels.me

Is a blogging network for Chelsea Fan blogs with user submitted content, run and controlled by TheChels.org group - owners of thechels.net, thechels.co.uk, thechels.biz, and thechels.tv,

Thechels.me was originally intended to be a social network but never really gained enough support or users compared to more traditional sites like twitter and facebook. This site aims to support bloggers and writers by providing an opprtunity to post short form content and links to their own sites to articles already written, we don't publish content automatically and everything has to be submitted by the user, thus guaranteeing opinions and fresh content from real fans you might not otherwise be aware of.

As a writer/blogger this offers a great opportunity to get your site some hits or your content published and read by likeminded fans and for the reader it can be seen as a curation of selected content relevant to you, in a quick and simple website that will work on all devices/browsers.

Article Types

There are two types of content suitable for this site. Linked articles or blog articles.

Linked articles are short posts linking to your own blog or content published elsewhere, we need the URL, the title and a brief introduction or description. these can be identifed by the bookmark icon at the start of the title

Articles need to be a certain format and you can see examples here for linked articles and here for the blog articles.

Submitting Content

There are several ways to submit blog articles

The quickest way to get something published is to open a github account and fork the repo which can be found at http://github.com/thechels/thechels.me, then create a markdown file and submit a pull request

The next and slowest way is to email editor@thechels.co.uk with either attaching a text document or sending a plain text email. Do not send Microsoft Word documents, they are as useful to us as the trophy cabinet at the Emirates. That is the sort of thing an Arsenal fan would do.

the attached document needs to include the information in the link article

Example blog article

A simple blog article requires the following information:

---

layout: post
title: Lukaku stats report
date: 2013-07-11 22:00:00
author: ChelseaStats
handle: twitter.com/ChelseaStats

---

And here is your full text article, we don't want any images, as they can be slow to load and this site is built for mobile devices as well as desktop computers. you can use html if you like.

or just space you content out for paragraphs.

if you are not sure how to code for the web just put in things like <bold>some bold text</bold> or <link> http://somewebsite.com ~ text displayed </link> and we'll do the rest.

Example linked article

A simple linked article requires the following information, and will not be published without it. The easiest thing to do is copy this into notepad or a text editor of your choice and change the details to suit, then email it to us

---

layout: post
title: Lukaku stats report
link: http://thechels.co.uk
date: 2013-07-11 22:00:00
author: yourtwittername


---

and here is brief description to really sell what you are writing about. You can use <i>italics</i>, <b>bold text</b> and other basic html if you know how. You should really read this, the information is frighteningly good, no wonder Jamie Carragher had to retire.

If you want an author page on our site thechels.me/yourname or you already have one then include the following line in your file.

---

categories: yourtwittername

---

Author Pages

Author pages need some basic info about you, as much or as little as you please, the minimum is your name:

---

layout: post
title: Your Name
url: http://thechels.co.uk
twitter: yourtwittername
googleplus: your author id
image: http://twitimg.com/yourtwitterimageurl
bio: some text about how long you haveve been a fan or where you watch games or how long you have been writing... anything
---

Other Information

All articles need your name and social media link/handle, twitter preferred, but google or Facebook welcome, we just want to credit you correctly and hopefully gain you followers/friends, comments and conversation with like minded fans.
